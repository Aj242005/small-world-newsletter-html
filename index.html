📸 Past Event Highlights (10th May)

Hand Built Pottery: Molding Dreams
Date: 10th May, 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Our “Hand Built Pottery” session was a clay‐tastic success! Couples and friends explored pinching, coiling, and slab techniques—then took home their one‐of‐a‐kind creations. The smiles (and muddy fingers!) said it all.

  • Over 30 pots molded by creative hands
  • Special gift: a small succulent planted in each finished piece
  • Participant feedback: “Loved the cozy vibe and the instructor’s tips!”

Bad Drawing: Unleash Your Awesomely “Bad” Artistry
Date: 10th May, 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM

We embraced imperfection and turned “bad art” into a joyful celebration! From hilariously abstract doodles to unexpected masterpieces, attendees laughed and bonded over some gloriously goofy sketches.

  • Fun icebreaker games (blindfold doodle challenge!)
  • Most “Outrageously Bad” drawing won a surprise gift voucher
  • Quote from a guest: “I’ve never laughed so much while drawing—instant stress‐buster!”

Boho Texture Painting: Transform Your Canvas
Date: 10th May, 12:00 PM – 2:00 PM

The “Boho Texture” workshop brought out everyone’s inner free spirit! Using palette knives, texture pastes, and earthy hues, each participant created a stunning layered artwork. The rustic ambiance at Barista perfectly complemented these creations.

  • Over 20 unique textured art pieces showcased afterward
  • “Best Use of Earthy Tones” award for one standout creation
  • Participant joy: “I can’t wait to hang this in my home!”
